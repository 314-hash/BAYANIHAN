🔍 Non-Technical Web3 Glossary

Soulbound Profile (SBT)

A digital identity card that belongs exclusively to you. Unlike standard tokens, it cannot be sold, traded, or transferred. This protects your reputation metrics from speculation and fraud.

Direct-To-Consumer (DTC)

Buying crops directly from local farmers without intermediate brokers. This ensures farmers earn fair returns and awards buyers a 3x BAYANI token incentive.

Escrow Contract

A secure digital lockbox. A client's funds are held safely by the contract and automatically released to the freelancer only after work milestones are submitted and approved.

Barangay DAO

Decentralized Autonomous Organization. A community democracy tool. Citizens stake tokens to boost voting weight on local infrastructure projects, with weight caps to maintain fair voting power.

Real-World Asset (RWA)

Fractional digital shares of physical community assets (like solar microgrids or crop warehouses). Return yields are distributed strictly as utility service discounts to comply with SEC rules.

Post-Quantum Keys (PQ)

State-of-the-art security keys. They safeguard your digital profile from future hacking by quantum computers. You can rotate and update them with a single click in the sidebar.
